    Original Limited Edition Watches

    Our Limited Edition G-Shock CasiOak watches are exclusively designed with creativity and originality in mind. Every dial, bezel, and index is customized and hand-painted with high-quality colors, resulting in a unique pattern for each watch. The paint is airbrushed, and the colors UV-resistant, including a pearlized finish that gives them the shimmering refractive effect with the best opacity.

    Small Wrists Get a New Hype with TICS's CasiOak Mini

    Featuring a five-color rainbow with more compact dimensions that watch enthusiasts will appreciate, the G-Shock CasiOak Mini is a smaller alternative to the GA-2100. The Mini Polar & Latte Rainbow are unisex, offering great comfort for those with slender wrists.
